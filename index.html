Creating a SOHO Network

For this assignment, I need to create a detailed diagram of a SOHO
(Small Office/Home Office) network, illustrating how the internet
reaches all of your wireless devices, step by step. This will involve showing
how the internet is connected to your modem, how the router distributes the signal,
and how the Wi-Fi network allows different devices to access the internet. The goal is
to provide a clear and comprehensive view of the network flow, ensuring
all components and connections are easily understood.


Story Flowchart

For this assignment, you’ll create a story flowchart set in a zombie apocalypse,
where every choice you make can lead to survival or disaster. If you choose correctly,
you move forward on your journey. But if you make the wrong choice, it’s "GAME OVER,"
and you’ll have to restart the entire game. The fate of your character is in your hands,
choose wisely, or risk losing everything.
