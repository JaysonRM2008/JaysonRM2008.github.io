Projects I did at STEAM:

Personal Website: Vision Board

For this assignment, I need to create a vision board that outlines what I would like to add into my website designs. It’s an opportunity to explore and visualize the layout and style I want to create, helping to guide my decisions as I build and develop the website further.

Creating a SOHO Network

For this assignment, I need to create a detailed diagram of a SOHO (Small Office/Home Office) network, illustrating how the internet reaches all of your wireless devices, step by step. This will involve showing how the internet is connected to your modem, how the router distributes the signal, and how the Wi-Fi network allows different devices to access the internet. The goal is to provide a clear and comprehensive view of the network flow.

Story Flowchart

For this assignment, you’ll create a story flowchart set in a zombie apocalypse, where every choice you make can lead to survival or disaster. If you choose correctly, you move forward on your journey. But if you make the wrong choice, it’s "GAME OVER," and you’ll have to restart the entire game.

Private Doctors Office Floor Plan

For this assignment I have to make a floor plan for a private doctors office. I had to add cameras in the hallway without having a blind spot, and I can’t put them inside offices since it is illegal. I also had to add key card access, a buzz door, BitLocker, a cable lock, and a server room.

Business Showcase

This project was no ordinary project. For this assignment we had to make a business and then code a website about it. It was a group of four. After we finished the website, the Brooklyn STEAM Center had their showcase where they invited their partnerships to the school and some colleges.
